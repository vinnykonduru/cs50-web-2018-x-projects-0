Quinoa Salad

Quinoa is really easy to cook,you just need to stir it into boiling water,cover and simmer over medium heat until done
This salad is quite simpl,you need to cook quinoa and add rest of the ingrediants.

Ingrediants



ingrediants measurement
1/2 cup uncooke quinoa 100g
1/4 cup corn 35g
1/4 cup chopped carrots 40g
12 cherry tomatoes 175g
12 black olives 20g
1 avocado 30g
olive oil 10g

Directons

  • Add avocado,cucumber,onion,tomatoes,cilantro into a bowl.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add extra virgin olive oil and toss it.
  • Adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Serve and Enjoy!!!
