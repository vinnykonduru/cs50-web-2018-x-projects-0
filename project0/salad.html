Cucumber Tomato Avocado Salad

Ingrediants

Serves 2 people

  • 2 Avocados - sliced into small cubes.
  • 1 english cucumber - sliced.
  • 2 Campari tomatoes - sliced in small pieces.
  • 1 onion - sliced.
  • 1 cup cilantro - chopped.
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil or to taste.
  • 1/2 teaspoon himalayan pink salt or to taste.
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper or to taste.

Preparation

  • Add avocado,cucumber,onion,tomatoes,cilantro into a bowl.
  • Season with salt and pepper.
  • Add extra virgin olive oil and toss it.
  • Adjust seasoning as needed.
  • Serve and Enjoy!!!
