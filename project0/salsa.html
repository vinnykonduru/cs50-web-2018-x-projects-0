Spicy Salsa

Secrets of making the best Homemade salsa Reciepe! This restaurant style salsa is loaded with flavor,has an amazing texture, and a sectret ingrediant.
serves 5 people

Ingrediants

  • 2 ripe tomatoes, cored and quartered
  • 1/2 onion, peeled and quartered
  • 1 garlic clove,peeled
  • 1 1/2 jalapenos, stemmed and seeded
  • 1/2 cup freshcilantro
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon himalayan pink salt or to taste.
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 7 ounces crushed tomatoes(1/2 can)
  • 3 diced green chillies,medium or hot

Instructions

  1. Place the fresh tomatoes,onion,garlic,jalapenos,green chillies,cilantro,lime juice,cumin,salt in a food processor. pulse until contents are fine and well blended.
  2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes. Puree until mostly smooth. Refrigerate until ready to serve


Reciepe Notes:

If refrigerated in an airtight container,this salsa should last 10-12 days..
