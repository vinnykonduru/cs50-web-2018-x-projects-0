3 Ingrediant Cookie

Ingrediants

* Made with just 3 ingrediants(not counting water and salt)
*vegan(dairy-free and egg-free)
*Gluten-free
*Oil-free

  • 1 cup blanched almond flour(not almond meal)
  • 3 tablespoons coconut powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium nowl, whisk the almond flour, coconut sugar, baking powder and salt with no lumps. Add water and mix until it forms firm cookie dough.
  3. make small balls with cookie dough and place on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes untill golden brown.
  5. Remove from the oven and cool for 10 minutes on the baking sheet.Transfer cookies to a cooling rack and cool completely...and ready to serve...!
